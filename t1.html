Durock T1

Durock T1 switches have a medium to large tactile bump at the top of the keypress. This bump provides good feedback to the user - you can immediately tell when the keypress is registered.

  • Tactile switch
  • 67g spring
  • Acuation Point: 2mm
  • Total Travel: 4mm
